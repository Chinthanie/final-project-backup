Journey to Design

While completing a psychology degree at Macquarie University, I found myself yearning for a creative outlet. I began considering the connection between creativity and the human mind. In my search I found Communication Design.

This instigated my enrolment at Swinburne University in Melbourne. By building my design knowledge and skills over the next four years and graduating with First Class Honours, I came to appreciate the intimate relationship between psychology and design. These range from the relevance of Gestalt principles and the aesthetic qualities of effective design to the relevance of cognitive and behavioural science in marketing, design strategy and crafting the user experience.

While I have a keen interest in advertising, branding and packaging where understanding audience psychology to motivate and persuade is vital, my passion is in UI/UX. This has lead me to join the team at Hoyne Design as a UI Designer.
